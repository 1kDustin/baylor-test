Our story

We have been serving Navasota, Grimes County, and surrounding areas for 118 years!

In the early 1900’s Joe N. Baylor had a dream. His dream was to open a lumber yard in Navasota, Texas. This dream became reality around 1906. Hard work and a passion for the lumber industry led to continued success and growth for his company. Baylor Lumber Company continued it’s strong growth through WWI, The Great Depression, WWII, and even Y2K. Today, over 110 years later, that same passion continues with new owners (not so new to Baylor Lumber) Vickie Kolbasinski 30 years & Gerald Schomburg 24 years. With these combined years of experience and dedication and a very helpful, knowledgeable, and friendly staff, Baylor Lumber today remains as strong as ever. And more growth is coming soon!
